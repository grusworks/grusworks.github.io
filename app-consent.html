Consent to Personal Data Processing

Last updated: April 2026

By using the WeeksSavings mobile application (the "App"), I give my consent to the processing of the following personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws:

Data Processed

  • Username
  • Email address
  • App usage data (challenges, payments, progress)

Purposes of Processing

  • User identification within the App
  • Enabling data saving and synchronization features
  • Sending push notifications about challenge status

Processing Methods

Data is stored on Supabase servers (Frankfurt, Germany) using encryption.

Retention Period

Until the user deletes their account or submits a written request for deletion.

Your Rights

You may withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your account via Profile → Delete Account, or by contacting us at contact@grus.works
