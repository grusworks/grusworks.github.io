Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Grus Works ("we", "us") developed the WeeksSavings app. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Who We Are

Data controller: Grus Works
Contact: contact@grus.works

2. Data We Collect

When signing in with Google:

  • Email address
  • First and last name

Data you create in the app:

  • Names and parameters of savings challenges
  • Payment history (amounts, dates, statuses)
  • Savings progress and statistics

When using the feedback form:

  • Message text and category
  • Email address (if provided)
  • App version

Guest mode: Data is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Providing app features (creating and tracking savings challenges)
  • Syncing data across devices
  • Responding to your support requests
  • Improving the app

We do not sell your data to third parties and do not use it for advertising.

4. Where Data Is Stored

Your data is stored on Supabase servers in the EU (Frankfurt, Germany) region, in compliance with GDPR requirements.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — delete all your data via "Delete Profile" in Settings
  • Restriction — restrict processing of your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise your rights: contact@grus.works

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time via Profile → Settings → Delete Profile. After deletion, all data is permanently removed from our servers.

8. Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS) and Row Level Security in the database — each user can only access their own data.

9. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Policy Changes

We will notify you of significant changes through the app. The current version is always available at this page.

11. Contact

Privacy inquiries: contact@grus.works
